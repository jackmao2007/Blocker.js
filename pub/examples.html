Setting up trigger methods, action methods

Click and hold the colored area for two seconds, then enter password 123123 to show blocked content
This is revealed by clicking and holding the blocked area, then entering password "123123"
------------------------------------------------------------
Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.

JS 

      
        let blockDemo2 = Blocker("#block-demo2").block("orange", "blockerjsHold", {holdDuration: 2000})
        blockDemo2.setShowAction({mode: "password", password:"123123"})

Setting up reveil methods, after effects

Click the colored area to show blocked content
This is revealed by clicking the blocked area, the colored block will slide and disapear, the reveil contents will shake
------------------------------------------------------------
Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.

JS 

      
        let blockDemo3 = Blocker("#block-demo3").block("lightblue")
        blockDemo3.setAfterEffect({mode: "tremble", time: 2000})
        blockDemo3.setShowMethod({mode: 'scratch', scratchSize: 15, percentReveil: 85,  color: 'lightblue'})

Putting it all together

Click the blocked area 5 times, and solve the puzzle to show blocked content, see code for puzzle solution
This is revealed by clicking the blocked area 5 times, and solving the puzzle. The content will be hidden again in 2 seconds
------------------------------------------------------------
Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.

JS 

      
        let allDemo = Blocker("#all-demo").block("orange", "blockerjsMultiClick", {multiClicks: 5})
        allDemo.setShowAction({mode: "puzzle",color:"lightblue", trueMap: [[true, false,false,false],
                                                                           [true, false,false,false],
                                                                           [false, true,false,false],
                                                                           [false, false,true,false]]})
        allDemo.setShowMethod({mode: 'slide', color: 'orange'})
        allDemo.setAfterEffect({mode:"recover", time: 2000})
